Our story

Making space for women who've made space for everyone else

Come In Colour is a Berlin-based community offering donation-based art therapy workshops for women 40 and over — navigating stress, anxiety, divorce, migration, unemployment, and the quiet weight of lives lived largely for others.

Founded by two women — one with a background in AI and creative technology, one in art therapy — we bring together the healing power of art, the warmth of sisterhood, and the innovation of human-centred creativity.

You don't need to be an artist. You just need to show up.

  1. Art therapy

    Evidence-based, trauma-informed creative practice. No skill required — only your presence.

  2. Sisterhood

    Small circles of women who return together. Trust built slowly, held gently, lasting long after the session ends.

  3. AI & creativity

    Berlin's only art therapy circle using AI as a creative mirror — human warmth and technology, together.

You already know how to heal.
You just needed somewhere to do it.
Who this is for

Every woman carrying more than anyone thought to ask about

  • Newly arrived

    Rebuilding identity in a new language, a new system, a new life. You belong here even before Berlin feels like home.

  • In transition

    Divorced, separated, or starting over. Women whose map changed and who are finding a new route.

  • Carrying it all

    Working mothers, single parents, carers — women who give everything and have never been asked what they need.

  • Professionally invisible

    Skilled and experienced, but whose qualifications or language make them feel unseen at work.

  • Quietly searching

    Women who woke up one day not knowing who they are anymore — and are brave enough to find out.

  • Simply here

    No crisis required. Just a hunger for a room to breathe, create, and belong.

What we offer

Six workshops. One unfolding journey.

  1. 01

    The Quiet Room

    Slow, meditative art-making. No outcomes, no performance — just the brush and you.

  2. 02

    Roots & Routes

    Visual identity maps for women between worlds. Your language, your colours, your story.

  3. 03

    The Unsent Letter

    Write what you never got to say. Then paint over it, tear it apart, or let it go.

  4. 04

    Sisterhood Portrait

    Draw each other. See and be seen — really seen — for the first time in a long time.

  5. 05

    AI + Me

    Artificial intelligence as a creative mirror. A dialogue between your emotion and a machine.

  6. 06

    The Long Table

    One canvas, all of us. A communal painting ritual followed by a shared meal.

— what we believe —

We believe every woman carries a colour the world never asked to see.

We believe you don't need to be an artist to make something true.

We believe pain deserves a better language than diagnosis.

We believe migration is not a deficit. It is a second palette.

We believe sisterhood is the most durable infrastructure there is.

We believe you already know how to heal. You just needed somewhere to do it.

So come in. Come in colour.
